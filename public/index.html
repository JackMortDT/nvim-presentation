Origins of NVIM

  • According to GitHub(where the codebase of NVIM is), the first commit of this project came on january 31 by a Brazilian named Thiago Padilha.

Motivations behind development

  • Frustration with limitations of existing editors.
  • Desire for a more extensible and community-driven approach.
  • Recognition of the importance of collaboration and open-source principles.

So, why I choose NVIM over other editors? Let’s explore its key advantages

  • Efficiency in performance
  • Modular and extensible architecture
  • Great community support
